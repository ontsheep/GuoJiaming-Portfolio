Selected Projects 部分项目

Coelacanth（2025 · Hand-drawn Animation）
•Created a hand-drawn black-and-white animation exploring themes of life, evolution, and genetics.
•Developed the narrative starting from the coelacanth species and its long evolutionary history.
•Produced frame-by-frame animation using FlipaClip and completed editing with digital tools.

World TELE（2021 · Artist Book）
•Created a handmade book documenting the TELE world and its skeletal underworld.
•Illustrated the narrative from the viewpoint of the explorer Grandbe to guide audience immersion.
•Crafted the book using cowhide leather, paper, cardboard, white thread, and adhesives.

腔棘鱼（2025 · 手绘动画）
•创作黑白手绘动画，探索生命、进化与遗传主题。
•以腔棘鱼物种及其漫长进化史为起点展开叙事。
•运用FlipaClip制作逐帧动画，并通过数字工具完成后期剪辑。

World TELE（2021 · 手工书）
•手工制作记录TELE世界及其骨骼地狱的。
•以探险家Grandbe的视角绘制叙事插图，引导观众沉浸体验。
•采用牛皮革、纸张、硬纸板、白线及粘合剂手工制作成册。
