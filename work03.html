The Oasis Five 绿洲五侠

2021 · Photography Series 2021 · 摄影作品系列

Overview 概述

A series of portrait photographs taken in Xiamen's Shapowei district, capturing the myriad flavours of life and the journey of human growth.

这一系列人像拍摄于厦门沙坡尾，记录不同个体在城市空间中的生命气息与成长轨迹。

Context 背景

This series was created for a first‑year portrait course. I photographed five people of different ages in Shapowei, observing their everyday presence within the same environment.

这组作品是我大一人像课的作业。我在厦门沙坡尾拍摄了五位不同年龄的人物，记录他们在同一环境中的日常状态。

Process 过程

The portraits were shot on site using a camera, then refined in Photoshop through basic colour and contrast adjustments.

人像在沙坡尾实地拍摄，并在 Photoshop 中进行色彩与对比度的基础调整。
