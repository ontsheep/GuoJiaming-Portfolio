World TELE 世界 特乐

2021 · Artist Book 2021 · 手工书

Overview 概述

A handmade book about the world of TELE, chronicling the skeletal underworld and an adventure tale.

一本关于特乐世界的手工书，记录骨骼地下世界与一段冒险故事。

Context 背景

The TELE world conceived by our team, where I was tasked with depicting the skeletal underworld. I adopted the perspective of the explorer Grandbe, using an adventure narrative to immerse audiences in the wondrous setting of the TELE world.

特乐世界由我们的团队共同构建，而我负责描绘骨骼地下世界。我以探险者贝爷的视角展开叙事，用一段冒险故事带领观众进入这个奇异的 TELE 世界。

Process 过程

The cover is crafted from cowhide leather, whilst the entire book is assembled using brown and white paper, cardboard, white thread and adhesive.

封面使用牛皮革制作，内页由棕色与白色纸张、纸板、白线和胶水手工装订完成。
